About me

As you've just read, my name is Tiago Cruzeiro and I'm a 27 year-old fella from Viana do Castelo. I'm also a software developer!

I'm your regular tech-lover, "gotta know how this works" guy. I try to keep up with everything new and have a passion for discovery and discussion of tech related topics, being a constant presence on forums and comment sections. As I also dabble in the art of writing, I eventually began writing about tech myself, focusing later on the video game market. I still write, though not as much as I would like, for a Portuguese video game portal, which I also co-admin - go check portugalgamers.pt!

On the more academic and professional side of my life, I attended the Master's in Informatics Engineering @FEUP, having worked on my Master Thesis @Fraunhofer AICOS.

I'm currently working as an Oracle BPM & SOA Consultant at eProseed (Porto office).

On my free time I enjoy swimming, sightseeing, playing video games and singing.
