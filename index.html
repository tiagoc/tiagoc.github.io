About me

As you've just read, my name is Tiago Cruzeiro and I'm a 24 year-old fella from Viana do Castelo, finishing my Master's on Informathics Engineering @FEUP.

I'm your regular tech-lover, "gotta know how this works" guy. I try to keep up with everything new and have a passion for discovery and discussion of tech related topics, being a constant presence on forums and comment sections. As I also dabble in the art of writing, I eventually began writing about tech myself, focusing later on the video game market. I still write, despite not as much as I would like, for a Portuguese video game portal, which I also co-admin - go check portugalgamers.pt!

On the more academic and professional side of my life, I'm very interested in computer graphics, web and mobile development. This last Summer I worked as a freelancer on an interesting project, doing full stack web development (with a focus on the front-end). Great experience in a multi-disciplinary and international team!

On my free time I enjoy swimming, sightseeing, playing video games and singing (choir boys ahoy!)
